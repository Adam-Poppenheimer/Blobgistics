Remarks
IntelligentPanelBase does several things. 1. It attempts to keep itself near but not on top of some position in the world. 2. It may try to keep itself on the screen but in the loose direction of its intended position. 3. It remains active until it is deselected, at which point it is deactivated. It's primarily used for displaying information about various gameplay elements after those elements have been selected.
